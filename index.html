About Me

Hi! I’m Karan Ashok Dukare. I am currently a first-year engineering student at Dr. D. Y. Patil Institute of Technology, pursuing my studies in the AIDS (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science) branch. My journey started in the beautiful town of Ranjangaon Ganpati, where I completed my education until 10th grade. Afterward, I moved to Pune to attend Sir Parashurambhau College for my junior college.

Education

Although I consider myself an average student, I am determined to give my best in my engineering studies. My transition from a small town to Pune has taught me the value of adaptation and perseverance, shaping me into a more focused individual.

A Personal Story

One of the most memorable experiences of my life took place during my 10th grade in Ranjangaon Ganpati. I had been struggling with mathematics for months, and it was the subject that worried me the most during the board exams. One day, my teacher noticed my struggles and offered extra help after school hours. With her guidance and my relentless practice, I managed to not only pass but also score one of the highest marks in the subject. That experience taught me the power of persistence and how the right support can help us overcome any challenge. It became a cornerstone for my belief in hard work and determination.
